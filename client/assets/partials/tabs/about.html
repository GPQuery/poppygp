About

poppyGP is an online community-driven video channel and IRC chatroom single-page web application created and maintained by Joho (Jonathan Ho).

Hardware

A resilient Windows 7 dedicated media server is powered by a dual-core AMD Athlon II X2 central processing unit delivering clocked speeds as high as 3.0 GHz and assisted by a 500MHz ATI Radeon HD 4200 integrated graphics processor. A formidable 5.0 GB of DDR3 SDRAM occupies three of the four available 240-pin DIMMs on the motherboard.

Software

Video broadcasting made possible by VLC Media Player and Open Broadcaster Software.

Library

Accumulated over the course of four years of meticulous searching and encoding from source VHS recordings, the video library consists of quality English-language broadcasts: full races and race highlights; practice, qualifying, and warmup sessions; official season reviews; and full session onboard footage.

Other racing series that may be seen include: GP2/3, Formula 2/3, Formula 3000, and Formula Renault 3.5; World Endurance and World Sportscar Championships.

Its entirety is stored on a SPAN/BIG ("JBOD") configuration of various Serial ATA hard disk drives, the sum of which is just in excess of 3TB.
