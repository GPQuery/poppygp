An Adblock extension or plugin for your browser is highly recommended. While I personally will not include any sort of advertising that are commonly found on stream sites, the streaming service may choose to do so.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the commentary in German/French/Japanese/Italian?

Depending on the era, the network coverage of the video in session may not be complete. For instance, ITV coverage in the 1990s was interrupted by advertisements/commercials. For older races, the footage may simply be unwatchable due to the medium on which the video was stored, e.g. deterioration of VHS tapes.

Whatever the reason may be, the person responsible for encoding the video in question may opt to "patch" these missing bits with quality coverage from other networks.

I do my best to select video files that meet a number of minimum requirements regarding quality of video/audio, but I do mess up on occasion simply because of the sheer amount of time it takes to do so.

Where can I download these?

Unfortunately due to the terms under which I obtain the majority of these files, regrettably, I am prohibited from sharing any content or details with any outsiders.

However, most races after the 1980s are available on the greater internet if you look hard enough!

Can I request a race/session?

Of course. However, due to the fact that there is currently no official or standard line of communication, I cannot guarantee that your request will be fulfilled.

A requests system is in the works.
